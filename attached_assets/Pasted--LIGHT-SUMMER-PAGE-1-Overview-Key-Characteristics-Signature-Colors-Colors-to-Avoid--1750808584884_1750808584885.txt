## LIGHT SUMMER

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** You are a Light Summer, blessed with soft, cool coloring that requires gentle, understated hues. Your features have a delicate quality with cool undertones - typically light hair with ashy tones, fair skin with pink or blue undertones, and soft-colored eyes. Your palette consists of light, cool, muted colors: powder blue, soft pink, lavender, and sage green. These colors enhance your natural ethereal quality without overwhelming your gentle features. Avoid warm, bright, or dark colors that will clash with your cool undertones and overpower your naturally subtle beauty.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Cool (pink or blue undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** Low (gentle blending between features)
- **Chroma:** Light & Muted (soft, gentle colors)
- **Value:** Light to Medium-Light (avoiding dark colors)

**Signature Colors:**

- Powder Blue (`#B0E0E6`) - Your most flattering light color
- Soft Pink (`#F8BBD9`) - Delicate and feminine
- Lavender (`#E6E6FA`) - Light and ethereal
- Mint Green (`#F0FFF0`) - Fresh and gentle
- Light Gray (`#D3D3D3`) - Your perfect neutral
- Dusty Rose (`#D8BFD8`) - Soft and romantic
- Periwinkle (`#CCCCFF`) - Light and serene
- Soft Aqua (`#B0F2F2`) - Cool and refreshing

**Colors to Avoid:** Dark Colors, Black, Deep Navy, Bright Colors, Orange, Coral, Warm Yellow, Golden Brown, Rust, Bright Red, Electric Blue, Deep Purple, Pure White

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your Light Summer palette consists of the lightest, most delicate colors with cool undertones. These colors have an ethereal, almost translucent quality that mirrors your gentle coloring. The palette includes the palest blues, softest pinks, and lightest greens, all with a muted, powdery finish.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Cool (blue-based undertones)
- **Saturation:** Low (very soft and muted)
- **Value Range:** Light (avoiding medium to dark colors)
- **Intensity:** Delicate and ethereal

**Your Light Summer Wardrobe:** Your wardrobe should be built around light, airy colors that create a soft, feminine look. Light gray serves as your primary neutral instead of black or navy. Patterns should be delicate and small-scale - think tiny florals, soft stripes, or gentle textures in your light colors. Avoid anything heavy, dark, or high-contrast.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Silver** - Light and delicate
- **White Gold** - Soft and refined
- **Platinum** - Understated luxury
- **Light Pewter** - Soft and muted

**Jewelry Style:** Choose delicate, feminine pieces with intricate details. Your jewelry should be as light and airy as your coloring. Think fine chains, small pearls, and delicate gemstones.

**Watches:** Light metals with small faces. Soft leather bands in light colors like powder blue or soft pink.

**Glasses:** Light-colored frames in silver, light gray, or soft pastels. Avoid dark or heavy frames.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** Light Summers typically have naturally light brown to medium blonde hair with ashy undertones.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Ash Blonde** - Natural and light
- **Cool Light Brown** - Gentle depth
- **Platinum Blonde** - Very light and cool
- **Silver/Gray** - Elegant and soft
- **Cool Medium Blonde** - Light with cool tones

**Colors to Avoid:** Dark colors, golden blonde, warm brown, auburn, copper, or any warm undertones.
