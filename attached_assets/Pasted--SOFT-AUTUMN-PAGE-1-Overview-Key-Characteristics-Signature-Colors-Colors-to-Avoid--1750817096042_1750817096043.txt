## SOFT AUTUMN

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** As a Soft Autumn, you possess gentle, muted coloring with warm undertones that creates a naturally harmonious appearance. Your features are characterized by low contrast and warm-neutral tones - typically hair with golden or ashy highlights, skin with warm undertones, and eyes that often appear hazel or have mixed colors. Your ideal palette draws from autumn's most subtle colors: sage green, dusty rose, warm taupe, and soft brass. These muted, warm colors enhance your natural softness and complement your gentle coloring perfectly. Stay away from bright, cool, or high-contrast colors that will overwhelm your naturally understated beauty and appear too harsh against your refined features.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Warm-Neutral (golden with some cool influence)
- **Contrast Level:** Low (gentle blending between features)
- **Chroma:** Muted & Soft (gentle, toned-down colors)
- **Value:** Medium (avoiding light or dark extremes)

**Signature Colors:**

- Sage Green (`#9CAF88`) - Your perfect muted green
- Dusty Rose (`#D8BFD8`) - Soft and romantic
- Warm Taupe (`#8B7765`) - Your ideal neutral
- Soft Brass (`#B8860B`) - Gentle metallic tone
- Mushroom (`#DDD0C0`) - Your soft brown
- Soft Rust (`#CD853F`) - Muted and warm
- Khaki (`#F0E68C`) - Your light neutral
- Soft Olive (`#BDB76B`) - Muted and sophisticated

**Colors to Avoid:** Bright Colors, Pure White, Black, Electric Blue, Hot Pink, Orange, Bright Red, Pure Colors, High Contrast Combinations, Cool Grays

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your Soft Autumn palette consists of muted, gentle colors with warm undertones. These colors have a sophisticated, understated quality that creates beautiful tonal combinations. The palette includes colors that look like they've been softened with a warm, gray filter - creating harmony that never competes with your natural coloring.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Warm-neutral (golden with muted quality)
- **Saturation:** Low (heavily muted)
- **Value Range:** Light-medium to medium (avoiding extremes)
- **Intensity:** Gentle and harmonious

**Your Soft Autumn Wardrobe:** Your wardrobe should focus on tonal dressing and sophisticated, muted combinations. Taupe and mushroom serve as your perfect neutral bases. Create beautiful monochromatic looks using different shades of the same muted color family. Your patterns should be soft and blended - think watercolor effects, subtle tweeds, or gentle textures.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Muted Gold** - Soft and warm
- **Rose Gold** - Gentle and feminine
- **Brass** - Warm but not too bright
- **Bronze** - Muted and sophisticated
- **Antique Gold** - Soft, aged finish

**Jewelry Style:** Choose pieces with soft, muted finishes rather than highly polished metals. Your jewelry should blend harmoniously with your coloring rather than create contrast.

**Watches:** Muted metals with soft leather bands in taupe, sage, or dusty rose. Avoid shiny or high-contrast styles.

**Glasses:** Soft, muted frames in taupe, sage green, dusty rose, or soft gold. Avoid sharp contrasts or bright colors.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** Soft Autumns typically have naturally light brown to dark blonde hair with mixed warm and cool tones.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Soft Brown** - Natural and muted
- **Mushroom Brown** - Cool-warm balance
- **Dusty Blonde** - Muted and sophisticated
- **Soft Auburn** - Gentle red tones
- **Ash Brown** - Cool but not harsh
- **Soft Gray** - Elegant and natural

**Colors to Avoid:** Bright colors, high contrast, pure golden tones, or very dark colors.
