## LIGHT SPRING

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** You are a Light Spring, blessed with delicate, luminous coloring that requires gentle yet warm hues. Your features have a light, fresh quality with warm undertones - typically featuring light hair with golden tones, fair skin that may have a peachy glow, and bright, clear eyes. Your palette consists of soft, warm colors with plenty of light: peachy pinks, warm yellows, light corals, and soft greens. These colors enhance your natural luminosity without overwhelming your delicate features. Avoid dark, heavy, or overly cool colors that will overpower your gentle coloring and make you appear tired or harsh.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Warm (golden or peachy undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** Low to Medium (gentle, blended features)
- **Chroma:** Light & Clear (soft but fresh colors)
- **Value:** Light (avoiding medium to dark colors)

**Signature Colors:**

- Peach (`#FFCBA4`) - Your perfect warm color
- Light Coral (`#F08080`) - Fresh and flattering
- Warm Yellow (`#FFFF99`) - Sunny and gentle
- Mint Green (`#98FB98`) - Fresh and light
- Light Aqua (`#AFEEEE`) - Your best light blue
- Warm Pink (`#FFB6C1`) - Soft and feminine
- Cream (`#FFF8DC`) - Your ideal neutral
- Light Camel (`#F5DEB3`) - Warm and gentle

**Colors to Avoid:** Dark Colors, Black, Navy, Deep Purple, Burgundy, Forest Green, Cool Colors, Gray, Pure White, Bright Colors, Electric Shades

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your Light Spring palette consists of the lightest, most delicate warm colors. These colors have a fresh, airy quality with golden undertones that complement your luminous coloring. The palette includes colors that look like they've been kissed by sunlight - soft peaches, gentle corals, and warm pastels.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Warm (golden undertones)
- **Saturation:** Light (soft and delicate)
- **Value Range:** Light (avoiding medium to dark)
- **Intensity:** Fresh and luminous

**Your Light Spring Wardrobe:** Your wardrobe should be built around light, warm colors that create a fresh, youthful look. Cream serves as your primary neutral instead of white or beige. Patterns should be delicate and light - think small florals, soft stripes, or gentle textures in your light, warm colors.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Light Gold** - Delicate and warm
- **Yellow Gold** - Classic and feminine
- **Rose Gold** - Soft and romantic
- **Light Copper** - Warm and unique

**Jewelry Style:** Choose delicate, feminine pieces with warm metals. Your jewelry should be as light and airy as your coloring. Think fine chains, small pearls, and delicate gemstones in warm tones.

**Watches:** Light gold or rose gold with small faces. Soft leather bands in light warm colors like peach or light camel.

**Glasses:** Light-colored frames in gold, light tortoiseshell, peach, or soft warm colors. Avoid dark or heavy frames.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** Light Springs typically have naturally light blonde to light brown hair with golden undertones.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Golden Blonde** - Light and warm
- **Honey Blonde** - Sweet and natural
- **Light Golden Brown** - Gentle depth
- **Strawberry Blonde** - Fresh and light
- **Champagne Blonde** - Soft and luminous

**Colors to Avoid:** Dark colors, ash tones, platinum blonde, cool browns, or any hair color without warm undertones.
