## BRIGHT SPRING

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** You are a Bright Spring, characterized by your vibrant, clear coloring that demands equally vivid colors. Your natural features have a warm undertone with high contrast and exceptional clarity - think bright, sparkling eyes and hair with golden highlights. Your palette consists of the most saturated, energetic colors in the spectrum: true reds, electric blues, sunny yellows, and emerald greens. These colors mirror your naturally dynamic appearance and ensure you always look radiant and alive. Avoid muted, dusty, or overly dark colors as they will drain your natural vitality and make you appear washed out.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Warm (golden undertones with some cool clarity)
- **Contrast Level:** High (striking contrast between features)
- **Chroma:** Bright & Clear (most saturated colors)
- **Value:** Full Range (can wear light to medium-dark)

**Signature Colors:**

- Electric Blue (`#0080FF`) - Your most striking color
- True Red (`#FF0000`) - Bold and powerful
- Bright Yellow (`#FFFF00`) - Pure sunshine
- Emerald Green (`#50C878`) - Vibrant and fresh
- Hot Pink (`#FF69B4`) - Electric and fun
- Orange (`#FFA500`) - Energetic and bold
- Purple (`#8000FF`) - Bright and dramatic
- Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) - Clean and crisp

**Colors to Avoid:** Muted Colors, Dusty Tones, Beige, Taupe, Olive Green, Burgundy, Navy, Charcoal, Sage Green, Dusty Pink, Brown, Cream

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your Bright Spring palette features the most saturated, electric colors available with warm undertones. These are pure, undiluted hues with maximum clarity and energy. Your palette includes the brightest yellows, clearest reds, and most vibrant greens - all with an electric, almost neon-like quality that matches your dynamic energy.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Warm-neutral (golden with some cool clarity)
- **Saturation:** Maximum (brightest, most intense colors)
- **Value Range:** Light to medium-dark (avoiding very dark)
- **Intensity:** Electric and dynamic

### **PAGE 3: Clothing Overview**

**Your Bright Spring Wardrobe:** Your wardrobe should be built around high-impact, energetic pieces. You can wear head-to-toe bright colors that would overwhelm other seasons. White serves as your perfect light neutral. Your patterns should be bold and geometric with high contrast between colors. Think statement pieces and eye-catching combinations.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Bright Gold** - Polished and radiant
- **Yellow Gold** - Classic and shiny
- **Rose Gold** - Modern and bright
- **Silver** - For contrast and brightness

**Jewelry Style:** Choose bold, statement pieces with clean, modern lines. Your jewelry should be as dynamic as your coloring. Think large, architectural pieces in bright metals.

**Watches:** Bright metals with bold faces. Colorful bands in your signature colors work well.

**Glasses:** Bright-colored frames in your signature colors, or classic gold and silver. Bold, modern shapes work best.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** Bright Springs often have naturally medium blonde to light brown hair with golden highlights and high contrast against their features.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Bright Golden Blonde** - Vibrant and sunny
- **Auburn** - Rich and dynamic
- **Copper Red** - Bold and striking
- **Honey Blonde** - Bright and warm
- **Golden Brown** - Rich with golden highlights
- **Bold Fashion Colors** - Bright red, electric blue in streaks

**Colors to Avoid:** Muted colors, ash tones, dark colors without contrast, or any dull finishes.
