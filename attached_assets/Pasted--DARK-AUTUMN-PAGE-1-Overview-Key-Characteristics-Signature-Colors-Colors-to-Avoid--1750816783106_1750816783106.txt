## DARK AUTUMN

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** As a Dark Autumn, you possess deep, rich coloring with warm undertones that can carry the most sophisticated earth tones. Your features are characterized by depth and warmth with high contrast - typically dark hair, deep or striking eyes, and skin with warm undertones that may have golden or olive qualities. Your ideal palette draws from autumn's most intense colors: deep burgundy, forest green, dark chocolate, and burnt orange. These rich, deep colors enhance your natural intensity and complement your warm, dramatic coloring beautifully. Stay away from light, cool, or pastel colors that will wash out your naturally powerful presence and fail to match your sophisticated depth.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Warm (golden or olive undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** High (strong contrast between features)
- **Chroma:** Deep & Rich (darkest autumn colors)
- **Value:** Deep (avoiding light to medium colors)

**Signature Colors:**

- Deep Burgundy (`#722F37`) - Your most sophisticated color
- Dark Chocolate (`#654321`) - Rich and luxurious
- Forest Green (`#355E3B`) - Deep and natural
- Burnt Orange (`#CC5500`) - Warm and striking
- Espresso (`#3C2415`) - Your darkest neutral
- Deep Rust (`#B7410E`) - Bold and earthy
- Dark Olive (`#556B2F`) - Sophisticated green
- Warm Black (`#1C1C1C`) - Your alternative to true black

**Colors to Avoid:** Pure White, Pastels, Light Colors, Cool Blues, Pink, Lavender, Light Gray, Icy Colors, Bright Colors, Silver Tones

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your Dark Autumn palette consists of the deepest, richest colors with warm undertones. These colors have weight and intensity, creating an atmosphere of luxury and sophistication. Your palette includes the darkest oranges, deepest browns, and richest greens - all with a mysterious, powerful quality.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Warm (golden/olive undertones)
- **Saturation:** Deep and rich
- **Value Range:** Deep (avoiding light to medium values)
- **Intensity:** Powerful and sophisticated

**Your Dark Autumn Wardrobe:** Your wardrobe should exude richness and sophistication. Build around deep, warm colors that create a sense of luxury and power. Dark brown and espresso serve as your primary neutrals. Your patterns should be rich and complex - think deep florals, sophisticated plaids, or intricate designs in your signature colors.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Gold** - Rich and warm
- **Bronze** - Deep and sophisticated
- **Copper** - Warm and striking
- **Antique Gold** - Rich and textured
- **Brass** - Warm and vintage

**Jewelry Style:** Choose substantial, sophisticated pieces with rich details and warm metals. Your jewelry can be bold and dramatic to match your powerful coloring.

**Watches:** Gold or bronze with dark leather bands in brown, burgundy, or black. Sophisticated, substantial styles work best.

**Glasses:** Rich, warm frames in gold, dark tortoiseshell, burgundy, or dark brown. Bold, substantial frames complement your strong features.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** Dark Autumns typically have naturally dark brown to black hair with warm undertones.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Dark Brown** - Rich and natural
- **Espresso** - Deep and sophisticated
- **Dark Auburn** - Rich with red undertones
- **Mahogany** - Deep and luxurious
- **Dark Copper** - Bold and striking
- **Chestnut** - Rich brown with warmth

**Colors to Avoid:** Light colors, ash tones, cool browns, platinum, or any hair color with cool undertones.
