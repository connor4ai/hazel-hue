## BRIGHT WINTER

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** You are a Bright Winter, possessing striking, high-contrast coloring that can handle the most intense colors in the spectrum. Your features are characterized by cool undertones with dramatic contrast - often dark hair, pale skin, and bright, piercing eyes. Your palette consists of the most saturated, cool-toned colors: true red, royal blue, emerald green, and pure white. These bold colors complement your naturally dramatic appearance and ensure you look powerful and sophisticated. Avoid warm, muted, or dusty colors that will clash with your cool undertones and diminish your natural intensity.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Cool (blue or pink undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** Very High (dramatic contrast between features)
- **Chroma:** Bright & Clear (needs the most saturated colors)
- **Value:** Full Range (can wear both very light and very dark colors)

**Signature Colors:**

- Electric Blue (`#0080FF`) - Your most stunning color that makes you glow
- True Red (`#FF0000`) - Bold and powerful, perfect for making statements
- Emerald Green (`#00FF80`) - Vibrant and sophisticated
- Hot Pink (`#FF1493`) - Bright and energetic
- Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) - Crisp and clean
- Jet Black (`#000000`) - Deep and dramatic
- Bright Purple (`#8A2BE2`) - Regal and striking
- Lemon Yellow (`#FFFF00`) - Your brightest, clearest yellow

**Colors to Avoid:** Peach, Coral, Orange, Warm Yellow, Golden Brown, Olive Green, Dusty Colors, Muted Tones, Sage Green, Taupe, Beige, Cream, Rust, Terracotta

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your Bright Winter palette features the most saturated, electric colors available. These are pure, undiluted hues with maximum clarity and intensity. Your palette includes the brightest blues, the clearest reds, and the most vibrant greens. Unlike True Winter, you can handle some lighter values, but they must still be clear and bright. Your colors should always have a electric, almost neon-like quality.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Cool (blue-based undertones)
- **Saturation:** Maximum (brightest, most intense colors)
- **Value Range:** Full spectrum (light to dark, but always bright)
- **Intensity:** Electric and vivid

### **PAGE 3: Clothing Overview**

**Your Bright Winter Wardrobe:** Your wardrobe should be built around high-impact, statement pieces. You're the only season that can truly pull off electric blue as a neutral. Black and white create perfect contrast for you. Your patterns should be bold and geometric, with high contrast between colors. You can wear head-to-toe bright colors that would overwhelm other seasons. Think runway fashion and high-fashion editorial looks.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Bright Silver** - Polished and reflective
- **Platinum** - Pure and pristine
- **White Gold** - Modern and clean
- **Chrome** - Shiny and contemporary

**Jewelry Style:** Choose bold, statement pieces with clean geometric lines. Your jewelry should be as dramatic as your coloring. Think large, architectural pieces in bright metals.

**Watches:** Bright silver, white gold, or high-tech materials like ceramic or titanium. Avoid anything muted or vintage-looking.

**Glasses:** Bright silver, black, white, or even electric blue frames. Bold, modern shapes work best.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** Bright Winters often have naturally dark hair with high contrast against pale skin.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Jet Black** - Maximum contrast and drama
- **Platinum Blonde** - If going light, go all the way
- **Cool Dark Brown** - Rich and intense
- **Bold Fashion Colors** - Electric blue, bright purple, hot pink
- **Silver/White** - Striking and modern

**Colors to Avoid:** Any warm tones, golden highlights, auburn, copper, or muddy colors.

### **PAGE 6: Makeup**

**Your Bright Winter Makeup Palette:** You can handle the most dramatic makeup looks. Think bold eyes, bright lips, and high-contrast combinations that would look costume-like on others.

**Foundation:** Cool undertones only - pink or neutral bases **Lipstick:** Bright red, hot pink, electric purple, bright berry**Eyeshadow:** Electric blue, bright purple, silver, black, white, emerald **Blush:** Bright pink, cool rose, berry **Eyeliner:**Jet black, bright blue, purple, or white for drama
