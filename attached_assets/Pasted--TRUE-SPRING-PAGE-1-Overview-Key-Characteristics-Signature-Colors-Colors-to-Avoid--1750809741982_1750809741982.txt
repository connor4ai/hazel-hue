## TRUE SPRING

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** As a True Spring, you embody the essence of renewal and warmth with your golden undertones and medium contrast features. Your coloring is naturally warm and fresh, often featuring hair with golden or red highlights, warm-toned skin, and eyes that sparkle with golden flecks. Your ideal palette draws from nature's most vibrant spring colors: warm coral, golden yellow, grass green, and clear turquoise. These colors enhance your natural glow and complement your warm undertones perfectly. Stay away from cool, muted, or dark colors that will clash with your warm, bright natural coloring and diminish your radiant appearance.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Warm (golden or peachy undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** Medium (balanced contrast between features)
- **Chroma:** Clear & Warm (fresh, vibrant colors)
- **Value:** Light to Medium (avoiding very dark colors)

**Signature Colors:**

- Coral (`#FF7F50`) - Your perfect warm-cool balance
- Golden Yellow (`#FFD700`) - Enhances your natural warmth
- Grass Green (`#7CFC00`) - Fresh and vibrant
- Clear Turquoise (`#40E0D0`) - Your best blue-green
- Warm Pink (`#FFB6C1`) - Soft and flattering
- Orange Red (`#FF4500`) - Bold and energetic
- Cream (`#F5F5DC`) - Your ideal light neutral
- Camel (`#C19A6B`) - Your warm brown

**Colors to Avoid:** Black, Pure White, Cool Blues, Purple, Burgundy, Navy, Cool Gray, Dusty Colors, Muted Tones, Cool Pinks, Charcoal, Forest Green

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your True Spring palette consists of clear, warm colors that capture the essence of a perfect spring day. These colors are fresh and vibrant without being overwhelming, featuring golden undertones that complement your natural warmth. The palette includes the clearest yellows, warmest corals, and freshest greens - all with a sunny, optimistic quality.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Warm (yellow-based undertones)
- **Saturation:** Medium-high (clear and fresh)
- **Value Range:** Light to medium (bright and cheerful)
- **Intensity:** Fresh and vibrant

**Your True Spring Wardrobe:** Build your wardrobe around fresh, warm colors that reflect your natural vitality. Camel and cream serve as your neutral bases instead of black or gray. Your patterns should be fresh and lively - think cheerful florals, classic plaids, or geometric patterns in your signature colors. Avoid anything dark, muted, or with cool undertones.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Gold** - Your most flattering metal choice
- **Yellow Gold** - Classic and warm
- **Rose Gold** - Soft and feminine
- **Copper** - Warm and unique
- **Brass** - Vintage and warm

**Jewelry Style:** Choose warm, cheerful pieces with classic or nature-inspired designs. Your jewelry should reflect your fresh, optimistic energy.

**Watches:** Gold or rose gold with warm leather bands in brown, tan, or coral. Classic or sporty styles work well.

**Glasses:** Warm-toned frames in gold, tortoiseshell (warm version), camel, or coral. Avoid cool or dark frames.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** True Springs typically have naturally strawberry blonde to medium brown hair with golden or red highlights.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Golden Blonde** - Warm and sunny
- **Strawberry Blonde** - Fresh and natural
- **Auburn** - Rich and warm
- **Golden Brown** - Medium depth with warmth
- **Honey Blonde** - Sweet and warm
- **Copper Red** - Bold and vibrant

**Colors to Avoid:** Ash tones, cool browns, platinum blonde, black, or any hair color with cool undertones.
