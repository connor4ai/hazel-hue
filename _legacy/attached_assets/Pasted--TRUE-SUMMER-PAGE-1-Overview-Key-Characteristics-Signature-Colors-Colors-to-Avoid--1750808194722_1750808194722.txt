## TRUE SUMMER

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** As a True Summer, you possess classic cool coloring with medium depth and natural elegance. Your features are characterized by cool undertones with moderate contrast - often featuring hair with ashy tones, skin with pink or blue undertones, and eyes in cool shades of blue, green, or gray. Your ideal palette draws from summer's most serene colors: soft blue, dusty pink, lavender, and sage green. These colors complement your cool undertones perfectly and enhance your naturally refined appearance. Stay away from warm, golden, or overly bright colors that will clash with your cool coloring and make you appear harsh or tired.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Cool (blue or pink undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** Medium (moderate contrast between features)
- **Chroma:** Muted & Soft (gentle, toned-down colors)
- **Value:** Medium (avoiding very light or very dark extremes)

**Signature Colors:**

- Soft Blue (`#87CEEB`) - Your most harmonious color
- Dusty Pink (`#D8BFD8`) - Perfect for your cool undertones
- Lavender (`#E6E6FA`) - Serene and sophisticated
- Sage Green (`#9CAF88`) - Your ideal muted green
- Rose Brown (`#BC8F8F`) - Warm enough but still cool-toned
- Soft Navy (`#4F6D8E`) - Your best dark neutral
- Powder Blue (`#B0E0E6`) - Light and airy
- Mauve (`#E0B0FF`) - Elegant and refined

**Colors to Avoid:** Orange, Coral, Peach, Warm Yellow, Golden Brown, Rust, Terracotta, Bright Red, Electric Blue, Lime Green, Black, Pure White, Camel, Ivory

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your True Summer palette consists of soft, muted colors with cool undertones. These colors have a gentle, toned-down quality that never overwhelms your natural coloring. The palette includes the softest blues, the most delicate pinks, and gentle greens with gray undertones. Your colors should feel serene and calming, like a misty summer morning or twilight sky.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Cool (blue-based undertones)
- **Saturation:** Medium-low (soft and muted)
- **Value Range:** Light to medium (avoiding extremes)
- **Intensity:** Gentle and harmonious

### **PAGE 3: Clothing Overview**

**Your True Summer Wardrobe:** Build your wardrobe around soft, elegant colors that create a refined, sophisticated look. Navy blue (in its softer version) serves as your primary dark neutral. Soft grays and powder blues work beautifully as lighter neutrals. Your patterns should be gentle and flowing - think watercolor florals, soft plaids, or delicate geometrics in your signature colors. Avoid high contrast combinations or anything with warm undertones.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Silver** - Classic and elegant for your cool tones
- **White Gold** - Modern and sophisticated
- **Platinum** - Luxurious and perfectly cool
- **Pewter** - Soft and muted alternative

**Jewelry Style:** Choose refined, elegant pieces with soft curves and gentle details. Your jewelry should complement your understated elegance. Pearls are particularly beautiful on True Summers.

**Watches:** Silver, white gold, or soft leather bands in navy, gray, or dusty pink. Avoid bold or sporty styles.

**Glasses:** Silver, soft gray, navy, or dusty rose frames. Gentle, rounded shapes work best with your soft features.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** True Summers typically have naturally medium brown to dark blonde hair with ashy undertones.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Ash Brown** - Natural and sophisticated
- **Cool Dark Brown** - Rich without warmth
- **Ash Blonde** - Light and elegant
- **Soft Gray** - Graceful aging
- **Cool Light Brown** - Medium depth with cool tones

**Colors to Avoid:** Golden blonde, warm brown, auburn, copper, red highlights, or any hair color with golden undertones.
