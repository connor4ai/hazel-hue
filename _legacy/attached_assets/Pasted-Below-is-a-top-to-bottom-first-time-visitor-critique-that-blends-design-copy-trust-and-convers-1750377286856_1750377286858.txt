Cross-page issues & fixes
Category	Issue	Fix
Visual hierarchy	Brown headline + brown body + orange button = three competing primaries.	Establish one accent (orange) for CTAs/icons; darken brown to #664125 for all headings; body text lighter.
Spacing rhythm	Section paddings vary (48 → 80 px).	Standardize top/bottom padding to 64 px desktop, 48 px mobile.
Accessibility	Brown (#7A5637) on beige (#FFF8F2) fails WCAG AA in some spots.	Either darken brown or lighten background; retest for 4.5:1.
Performance	Hero palette graphic likely heavy; dotted SVG repeats in multiple sections.	Compress hero as ≤ 30 KB WebP; replace pattern with single CSS background.
Mobile UX	Primary CTA scrolls away; four-column layouts become tiny cards.	Make “Get My Analysis” sticky bottom bar; convert grids to swipe carousels on screens < 640 px.
