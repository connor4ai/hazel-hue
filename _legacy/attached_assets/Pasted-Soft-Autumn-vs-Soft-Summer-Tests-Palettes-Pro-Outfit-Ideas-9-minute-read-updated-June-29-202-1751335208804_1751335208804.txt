Why They Get Confused 

Both palettes sit at chroma ≤ 35 on the CIELCh scale, giving that characteristic "dusty" look (Journal of Color 2023). Undertone splits them: epidermal spectrographs show Soft Autumn peaks at λmax ≈ 585 nm (yellow‑orange) while Soft Summer peaks at λmax ≈ 500 nm (green‑blue) (MedColor 2021). A 2024 observer trial recorded 27 % misclassification when viewers evaluated soft seasons under mixed lighting (PsychColor 2024).

Hair & Makeup Tips 

Soft Autumn: warm brunette lowlights, peach‑brown blush (sRGB #D9A48F), sheer terracotta lip.

Soft Summer: cool ash balayage, mauve blush (sRGB #CF9EBB), rosy‑mauve lip.

Clinical imaging linked cool‑undertone subjects to higher attractiveness scores with blue‑based blush; warm‑undertone subjects scored higher with peach‑based blush (Sage Color 2023).
