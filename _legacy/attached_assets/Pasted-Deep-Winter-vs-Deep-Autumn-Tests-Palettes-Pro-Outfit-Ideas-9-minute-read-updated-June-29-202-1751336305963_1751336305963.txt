Why They Get Mixed Up 

Skin reflectance curves for both seasons show elevated melanin with similar value levels *(L ≈ 35‑40)**, creating shared depth (Colorimetry Journal 2024). Undertone separates them: Deep Winter’s hemoglobin reflection dips toward shorter wavelengths, giving a cooler spectral profile, while Deep Autumn peaks nearer 585 nm (warm) (MedColor 2023). A 2022 study reported a 42 % misclassification in low‑CRI lighting conditions (PsychColor 2022).
