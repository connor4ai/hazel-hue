## TRUE AUTUMN

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** You are a True Autumn, embodying the rich, warm essence of the harvest season with your golden undertones and natural depth. Your coloring is characterized by warmth and medium contrast - often featuring hair with red or golden highlights, skin with warm, golden undertones, and eyes in warm shades of brown, hazel, or green. Your palette consists of autumn's most classic colors: rust orange, golden yellow, olive green, and warm brown. These colors enhance your natural warmth and create a rich, earthy sophistication that's uniquely yours. Avoid cool, bright, or icy colors that will clash with your warm undertones and drain the life from your naturally golden beauty.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Warm (golden or orange undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** Medium (balanced contrast between features)
- **Chroma:** Rich & Earthy (deep, saturated colors)
- **Value:** Medium to Deep (avoiding very light colors)

**Signature Colors:**

- Rust Orange (`#B7410E`) - Your signature autumn color
- Golden Yellow (`#FFD700`) - Rich and warm
- Olive Green (`#808000`) - Earthy and sophisticated
- Warm Brown (`#8B4513`) - Your perfect neutral
- Deep Orange (`#FF8C00`) - Bold and energetic
- Forest Green (`#228B22`) - Rich and natural
- Burnt Sienna (`#A0522D`) - Warm and earthy
- Cream (`#F5F5DC`) - Your light neutral

**Colors to Avoid:** Black, Pure White, Cool Blues, Purple, Pink, Cool Gray, Icy Colors, Pastels, Navy, Cool Greens, Silver Tones, Bright Colors

**Palette Description:** Your True Autumn palette consists of rich, warm colors that capture the essence of autumn foliage. These colors are deep and saturated with golden undertones that perfectly complement your natural warmth. The palette includes the richest oranges, deepest golds, and most sophisticated browns - all with an earthy, grounded quality.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Warm (golden/orange undertones)
- **Saturation:** Rich (deep and saturated)
- **Value Range:** Medium to deep (avoiding light values)
- **Intensity:** Earthy and sophisticated

### **PAGE 3: Clothing Overview**

**Your True Autumn Wardrobe:** Build your wardrobe around rich, warm colors that reflect the beauty of autumn. Brown serves as your primary neutral instead of black or gray. Your patterns should be rich and organic - think paisleys, warm florals, or ethnic prints in your signature colors. Embrace textures like wool, suede, and corduroy that enhance your earthy aesthetic.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Gold** - Your most flattering metal choice
- **Brass** - Warm and vintage
- **Copper** - Rich and earthy
- **Bronze** - Deep and sophisticated
- **Antique Gold** - Rich and textured

**Jewelry Style:** Choose substantial pieces with warm metals and organic designs. Your jewelry should reflect your rich, earthy nature. Think chunky gold pieces, amber, and warm gemstones.

**Watches:** Gold or bronze with warm leather bands in brown, tan, or cognac. Classic or vintage styles work well.

**Glasses:** Warm-toned frames in gold, warm tortoiseshell, brown, or cognac. Rich, substantial frames complement your features.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** True Autumns typically have naturally medium to dark brown hair with red or golden highlights.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Auburn** - Rich and classic
- **Golden Brown** - Warm and natural
- **Chestnut** - Deep and rich
- **Copper Red** - Bold and striking
- **Dark Golden Blonde** - Rich and warm
- **Mahogany** - Deep with red undertones

**Colors to Avoid:** Ash tones, cool browns, platinum blonde, black, or any hair color with cool undertones.
