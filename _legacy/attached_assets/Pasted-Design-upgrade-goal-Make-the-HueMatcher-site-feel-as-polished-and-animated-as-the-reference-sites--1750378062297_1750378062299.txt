Design-upgrade goal
Make the HueMatcher site feel as polished and animated as the reference sites — turn.io (clean gradients, subtle parallax) and elef.studio (layered depth, micro-animations) — while keeping our own warm-peach brand vibe and the conversion-centric structure we already have.

Top-level visual direction
• Keep our colour DNA (peach accent + espresso headings), but add a cool counter-tone (soft teal) and bright whites so elements pop.
• Replace flat beige fills with light radial gradients and blurred colour “blobs” in section corners — same style as Turn.io hero.
• Introduce real depth: 8-16 px drop-shadows on cards, cards lift + scale 1.02 on hover.
• Use solid or duotone icons in the teal accent; ditch thin outline icons.
• Adopt a consistent 64 px top/bottom section padding and a max-width container of 1100 px for desktop.

Motion & interaction (inspired by elef.studio)

Page load – fade-in hero headline from 0–100 % over 400 ms; CTA button slides up 20 px with spring ease.

Scroll cues – hero dot grid moves at 0.8× scroll speed (simple parallax).

Card hover – lift 8 px, shadow increases, icon gently floats (translateY −4 px).

Accordion – arrow rotates 180°, answer panel fades/expands (200 ms ease).

CTA pulse – primary buttons scale 1.05 for 250 ms every 6 s to draw the eye.
