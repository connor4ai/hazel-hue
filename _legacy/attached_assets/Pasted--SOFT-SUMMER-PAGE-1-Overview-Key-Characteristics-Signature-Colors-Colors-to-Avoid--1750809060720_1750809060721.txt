## SOFT SUMMER

### **PAGE 1: Overview, Key Characteristics, Signature Colors, Colors to Avoid**

**Overview:** You are a Soft Summer, characterized by your muted, cool coloring that creates a harmonious, understated beauty. Your features have low contrast with cool undertones - typically featuring hair, skin, and eyes that blend together in gentle, cool tones. Your palette consists of soft, muted colors with cool undertones: dusty blue, mauve, soft gray, and muted green. These colors enhance your natural subtlety and create a sophisticated, tonal look that's never overwhelming. Avoid bright, warm, or high-contrast colors that will clash with your muted natural coloring and appear too bold against your gentle features.

**Key Characteristics:**

- **Undertone:** Cool (blue or neutral-cool undertones)
- **Contrast Level:** Low (very low contrast between features)
- **Chroma:** Muted & Soft (gray-toned colors)
- **Value:** Medium (avoiding light or dark extremes)

**Signature Colors:**

- Dusty Blue (`#6495ED`) - Your perfect muted blue
- Mauve (`#E0B0FF`) - Sophisticated and soft
- Soft Gray (`#A9A9A9`) - Your ideal neutral
- Muted Green (`#8FBC8F`) - Gentle and natural
- Dusty Rose (`#D8BFD8`) - Soft and romantic
- Slate Blue (`#6A5ACD`) - Deeper but still muted
- Soft Taupe (`#C8B99C`) - Your warm neutral
- Pewter (`#899499`) - Cool and sophisticated

**Colors to Avoid:** Bright Colors, Pure White, Black, Orange, Coral, Bright Red, Electric Blue, Lime Green, Golden Yellow, Pure Colors, High Contrast Combinations

### **PAGE 2: Color Overview**

**Palette Description:** Your Soft Summer palette consists of muted, gray-toned colors with cool undertones. These colors have a sophisticated, understated quality that creates beautiful tonal combinations. The palette includes colors that look like they've been mixed with gray, creating a soft, harmonious effect that never competes with your natural coloring.

**Color Dimensions:**

- **Temperature:** Cool (blue-based undertones)
- **Saturation:** Very low (heavily muted)
- **Value Range:** Light-medium to medium (avoiding extremes)
- **Intensity:** Subtle and harmonious

**Your Soft Summer Wardrobe:** Your wardrobe should focus on tonal dressing and sophisticated color combinations. Gray serves as your perfect neutral base. Create beautiful monochromatic looks using different shades of the same color family. Your patterns should be soft and blended - think watercolor effects, subtle tweeds, or gentle ombré in your muted colors.

### **PAGE 4: Metals, Jewelry, Watches and Glasses**

**Best Metals:**

- **Silver** - Soft and muted
- **Pewter** - Perfect for your muted coloring
- **White Gold** - Understated elegance
- **Brushed Silver** - Soft finish that won't overwhelm

**Jewelry Style:** Choose pieces with soft, muted finishes rather than highly polished metals. Your jewelry should blend harmoniously with your coloring rather than create contrast.

**Watches:** Muted metals with soft leather bands in gray, dusty blue, or mauve. Avoid shiny or high-contrast styles.

**Glasses:** Soft, muted frames in gray, dusty blue, or mauve. Avoid sharp contrasts or bright colors.

### **PAGE 5: Hair Color**

**Natural Hair Colors:** Soft Summers typically have naturally medium brown to dark blonde hair with neutral to cool undertones.

**Best Hair Color Options:**

- **Ash Brown** - Natural and muted
- **Cool Medium Brown** - Soft depth
- **Dusty Blonde** - Muted and sophisticated
- **Soft Gray** - Elegant and natural
- **Mushroom Brown** - Cool and muted

**Colors to Avoid:** Bright colors, golden tones, warm brown, auburn, copper, or high-contrast colors.
