Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026 · Version 1.0

Hazel & Hue ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Amazon Cognito. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email as authorized by you.

Photos: When you request a color analysis, you upload a selfie photo. This photo is stored temporarily in Amazon S3 and is automatically deleted within 24 hours of upload. We do not retain your photos after analysis.

Analysis Results: Your seasonal color analysis results (palette, style guide, recommendations) are stored in our database and associated with your account so you can access them anytime.

Payment Information: Payments are processed through Apple In-App Purchase (iOS) or Google Play Billing (Android) via RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store your credit card number or payment details. We receive a purchase receipt and transaction ID for entitlement verification.

Email Address: You may optionally provide your email during the analysis processing screen so we can send you your results.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To perform AI-powered seasonal color analysis on your uploaded photo
  • To generate and deliver your personalized results (palette, style guide, makeup, hair, and jewelry recommendations)
  • To verify your purchase and grant access to the analysis service
  • To send you your results via email (if you provide your email)
  • To process refund requests

3. How We Process Your Photos

Your photo is analyzed using Amazon Bedrock (Claude AI) and Amazon Rekognition for face detection. The AI examines your skin undertone, eye color, hair color, and contrast level to determine your seasonal color type. Photos are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours. We do not use your photos for training AI models or any purpose other than generating your personal color analysis.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be processed by the following service providers solely for delivering our service:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud infrastructure (Cognito, S3, DynamoDB, Bedrock, Rekognition)
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase management and receipt validation
  • Apple / Google: Payment processing through their respective app stores

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption at rest for all stored data (DynamoDB, S3)
  • Encryption in transit via HTTPS/TLS for all communications
  • JWT-based authentication with Cognito
  • Presigned URLs for secure photo upload (time-limited, single-use)
  • Automatic photo deletion after 24 hours

6. Data Retention

  • Photos: Deleted automatically within 24 hours
  • Analysis Results: Retained as long as your account exists
  • Account Data: Retained until you request deletion
  • Purchase Records: Retained for accounting and refund purposes

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@hazelandhue.com.

8. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request its deletion. We do not sell personal information. Contact us to make a CCPA request.

9. European Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your data is your consent (provided when you create an account and upload a photo) and contractual necessity (to deliver the analysis service you purchased). You have the right to withdraw consent, request data portability, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Children's Privacy

Hazel & Hue is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:
